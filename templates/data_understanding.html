01

Dataset Description

The dataset contains 1,200 records of Bienes de Interés Cultural (BIC) from 10 Colombian cities across 8 departments. Each record integrates information from three sources: historical structure data, IDEAM climate variables, and OpenStreetMap urban variables.

02

Variable Analysis

The dataset includes 5 continuous numerical variables, 4 categorical variables, and 2 identifiers. The target variable is Nivel_Deterioro_Actual.

Numerical
Edad_Estructura_Anios
Age in years of the heritage building. Key historical variable.
Range: 50 – 385 years · Mean: 150.7
Numerical
Precipitacion_Anual_mm
Average annual precipitation in millimeters. Source: IDEAM stations.
Range: 472 – 2,260 mm · Mean: 1,202
Numerical
Humedad_Prom_Anual_pct
Average annual relative humidity percentage. Accelerates corrosion and mold growth.
Range: 51.7% – 99.4% · Mean: 73.4%
Numerical
PM10_Promedio_ugm3
Particulate matter concentration (μg/m³). Related to acid rain formation.
Range: -13.5* – 88.2 · Mean: 32.4
Numerical
Distancia_Via_Principal_m
Distance in meters to the nearest vehicular road. Indicates vibration risk.
Range: 2 – 672 m · Mean: 96.5 m
Categorical
Material_Principal
Dominant construction material: Piedra_Coralina, Ladrillo, Adobe_Tapia, Mamposteria, Piedra_Concreto, Piedra_Ladrillo.
6 categories · 190–219 records each
Categorical
Ciudad
Colombian city where the BIC is located: Medellín, Bogotá, Cali, Cartagena, Santa Marta, among others.
10 cities · 101–136 records each
Categorical — Target
Nivel_Deterioro_Actual
Target variable for the classification model. Represents the current structural deterioration state.
Low: 713 · Medium: 469 · High: 18

* PM10 negative values identified as anomalous data to be corrected in Data Engineering.
